Fun with the Family during the pandemic

My adult son and daughter came back to St. Louis at the beginning of the pandemic, and everyone worked from our home.

Sourdough Bread

This was my nephew's pet project. We acquired a sourdough starter from a friend, a cast iron pot, a banneton basket, and a few other accoutrements. He experimented with different ratios of flour to water, and went through 17 different trials. The rest of the family was happy to taste the trials!

Instagram

My daughter has always wanted me to open a bakery. She filmed me making two recipes and put the stories on Instagram.
